Elephants

Elephants are majestic and interesting creatures. They have a great memory and are symbol of luck.
Due to the ivory trade and poaching, the elephant population is rapidly decreasing.

Species of Elephant Location
Loxodonta africana sub-Saharan Africa
Elephas maximus South Asia & Souteast Asia

Fun Facts

  • Elephants are the largest land mammals, weighing up to 15,000 lb
  • Elephant tusks are made from ivory
  • Elephants laugh, cry, play, grieve, and throw temper tantrums
  • Elephants can't jump
